Academic Programs

Nurturing Minds, Shaping Futures

Kindergarten (Ages 3-5)

Our kindergarten program provides a nurturing environment where young children develop foundational skills through play-based learning. We focus on:

  • Early literacy and numeracy
  • Social and emotional development
  • Creative expression through art and music
  • Physical development and coordination
  • Basic computer skills

Our qualified teachers use the Ghana Education Service curriculum enhanced with Montessori-inspired activities.

Primary School (Grades 1-6)

Our primary program builds on the foundation laid in kindergarten, offering a comprehensive curriculum that includes:

  • English Language
  • Ghanaian Languages
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Citizenship Education
  • Information and Communication Technology
  • Creative Arts
  • Physical Education

We emphasize critical thinking, problem-solving, and the development of good study habits.

Junior High School (Grades 7-9)

Our JHS program prepares students for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) with a strong academic foundation:

  • Core subjects: English, Math, Integrated Science, Social Studies
  • ICT
  • French
  • Pre-Technical and Pre-Vocational Skills
  • Religious and Moral Education

We offer after-school tutoring and exam preparation classes to ensure student success.

Senior High School (Grades 10-12)

Our SHS program offers both General Arts and Science programs leading to the WASSCE:

General Arts

  • English Language
  • Mathematics (Core and Elective)
  • Government
  • Economics
  • Geography
  • Christian Religious Studies
  • History
  • Integrated Science
  • Social Studies

General Science

  • English Language
  • Mathematics (Core and Elective)
  • Social Studies
  • Integrated Science
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology

Our SHS boasts a 98% WASSCE pass rate with many students gaining admission to top universities.
