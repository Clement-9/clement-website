Admissions Process

Join Our Community of Learners

1

Inquiry

Visit our school or contact us to learn more about our programs and admission requirements.

2

Application

Complete the application form and submit required documents (birth certificate, previous report cards, etc.).

3

Assessment

Students may be required to take an entrance assessment (for certain grade levels).

4

Interview

An interview may be conducted with the student and parents/guardians.

5

Acceptance

Successful applicants receive an admission letter and fee payment details.

Admission Requirements

Kindergarten

  • Child must be at least 3 years old
  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • 2 passport photos
  • Medical report

Primary School

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • 2 passport photos
  • Previous school report cards (if applicable)
  • Transfer certificate (for continuing students)

JHS & SHS

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • 2 passport photos
  • BECE results (for SHS)
  • Previous school transcripts
  • Pass entrance examination

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Bright Future Academy offers several scholarship opportunities for outstanding students:

  • Academic Excellence Scholarship: For students with exceptional academic records
  • Sports Scholarship: For students with demonstrated talent in sports
  • Need-Based Financial Aid: For families with demonstrated financial need

To apply for scholarships, please submit a separate scholarship application along with supporting documents.
